# Automated-Plate-Delivery-Machine
**Current Status:** Hardware Migration in Progress
* Validated mechanical design and YOLOv8 model performance.
* Currently transitioning the computing core from **Raspberry Pi 4** to **NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano** to address computational bottlenecks encourntered during real-time SLAM integration.
---
This project presents the design, fabrication, and application of an Automated Plate Delivery Machine. It is a **Undergraduate Research Project** which was awarded the **Undergraduate Research  Grant** and funded by the **National Science and Technology Council (formerly MOST)**. The research have conducted at the National Taiwan Ocean University.

- **Research Institution**: Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan Ocean University
- **Advisor**: Prof. Chih-Yung Cheng
- **Researcher**: Hsin-Wei Lin
